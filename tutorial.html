Welcome to Psonic!

We're here to make remembering your passwords easier! Secure passwords may be long and complicated, but we'll generate some cool mnemonics to help you remember the important ones. Don't worry, they're all stored on your computer in an encrypted format that only you can access! To get started, let's set your master password, be sure to remember this one, as it's the only password we can't remember for you.
